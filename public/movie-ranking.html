The Best Movies According to Renz

My Top 3 Movies of all time

Interstellar

I like Interstellar because it makes me think beyond everyday life and imagine what’s possible in the universe. The film combines science, emotion, and sacrifice in a powerful way. I’m drawn to how it explores love, time, and survival, while showing the courage it takes to face the unknown for the future of humanity.

Avatar Films

I like Avatar films because they combine stunning visuals with a powerful story about connection, nature, and humanity. The world of Pandora feels alive and immersive.

Spiderman

My favorite superhero of all time!!
